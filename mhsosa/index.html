About Us

This is a campaign prepared with help of school and with the help of the school formed Shri Maheshwari Sr. Sec. School Old Student Association. In this, we MHS friends can create professional and business network and many other activities to bring closer the friends who studied together in school years ago, to re-enrich them with precious memories related to the school. We can connect seniors and juniors as well on a single platform from different different fields.

Friends, E-Directory / Web App of all passed out students from MHS is being created by MHSOSA. This form has been created to collect your basic information and work information. Everyone is requested to fill the form given below.
