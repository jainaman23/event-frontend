Maheshwari Sr Sec School Old Student Association (MHSOSA)

MHSOSA has been constituted by group of old students of MHS with the help and instruction of MHS school. In this, we MHS friends can create professional and business network and many other activities to bring closer the friends who studied together in school years ago. This is the platform to re-enrich them with precious memories related to the school. We can connect seniors and juniors as well on a single platform from different fields.

Purpose of this MHSOSA is to create a platform where old students can connect with each other and also establish a connection with the school so that old students and School can help each other. We are working to publish a E-directory of all Ex-students of MHS. We are also establishing a Corpus fund for the help of brilliant students of MHS (who need financial support) for their school fee and higher studies. All ex-students of MHS can contribute in this noble cause.
